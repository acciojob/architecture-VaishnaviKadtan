Architecture and Design

Classical architecture originated in ancient Greece and Rome. It emphasizes symmetry, proportion, and the use of columns such as Doric, Ionic, and Corinthian styles. These structures have influenced architectural design for centuries and remain iconic symbols of cultural heritage.
Modern architecture focuses on minimalism, innovation, and the use of new materials such as steel, glass, and reinforced concrete. Skyscrapers and contemporary buildings reflect advancements in engineering and sustainable design principles, shaping the skylines of cities around the world.
